Neon Breakout

How To Play

🕹️ Controls

Drag to move the paddle. Tap to launch stuck balls and fire lasers while Pew Pew is active.

🧱 Brick Types

Glass: 1 hit, Stone: 2 hits, Obsidian: 3 hits, Titanium: 4 hits, Cursed: indestructible.

⚡ Power-Ups

🎱 Chaos Mode: exactly 3 balls. ➡️ Thicc Stick: wider paddle. ✏️ Pencil Mode: narrower paddle.

🔥 Napalm: pass-through one-hit ball. 🔫 Pew Pew: dual lasers on tap. ❤️ One More Go: +1 life.

🧊 Chill Pill: slower ball. 🍯 Glue Trap: next paddle hit sticks ball until tap. 🛡️ Safety Net: bottom shield.

💣 The Nuke: next brick hit detonates a 3x3 area.

💡 Tips

Build combos by breaking bricks repeatedly before touching the paddle. Avoid Cursed blocks and route around them.

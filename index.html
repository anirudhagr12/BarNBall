Neon Breakout

How To Play

🕹️ Controls

Drag anywhere to move the paddle. Tap to launch, fire lasers, or release sticky balls.

🧱 Brick Types

Glass (1), Stone (2), Obsidian (3), Titanium (4), Cursed (unbreakable). Clear all non-cursed bricks to win.

⚡ Power-Ups

🎱 Chaos Mode — Splits play into exactly three active balls.
➡️ Thicc Stick — Paddle grows wider for easier catches.
✏️ Pencil Mode — Paddle shrinks, higher precision required.
🔥 Napalm — Ball passes through and one-shots breakable bricks.
🔫 Pew Pew — Tap to fire dual lasers for a short window.
❤️ One More Go — Adds one extra life instantly.
🧊 Chill Pill — Reduces active ball speed temporarily.
🍯 Glue Trap — Next paddle hit sticks a ball until tap launch.
🛡️ Safety Net — Bottom shield absorbs drops using visible charges.
💣 The Nuke — Next brick hit detonates a 3x3 blast.

💡 Tips

  • Use the cyan drag rail above the paddle as your thumb anchor.
  • Prioritize Titanium lanes last, clear Glass/Stone first for control.
  • Combo bonus grows when you avoid paddle contact between brick breaks.
